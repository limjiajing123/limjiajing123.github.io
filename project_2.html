MA4012 Design Of A Compact Autonomous Vehicle

The project aimed to create a versatile vehicle capable of executing crucial functions such as edge detection, target tracking, searching, and retrieval.

My responsibilities encompassed designing the chassis and ball collection system using Solidworks, integrating the VEX controller for enhanced functionality, planning the electrical system layout, implementing software algorithms for edge detection and target tracking, conducting thorough testing, and maintaining effective documentation for seamless collaboration. The vehicle participated in competitive scenarios against other teams, demonstrating its prowess in searching for and retrieving balls within an arena.
