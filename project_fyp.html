Final Year Project

Evaluating Navigation Among Crowd Algorithms For Shared Control Of A Robotic Wheelchair

In this project, I implmented collision avoidance (CA) algorithm named "reactive driving support" RDS to a simple model in Gazebo and rviz. Subeqeuntly, I will be comparing the CA algortihm with other CA algorithm based on metric.

Method behind RDS

RDS is extension of RVO(Reciprocal Velocity Obstacle) and ORCA (Optimal Reciprocal Collision Avoidance)

It uses the idea of the intersection of halfplanes and other constraints to select its velocity
